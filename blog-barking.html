Dear Diary: I Barked at Nothing and It Was Important

You'll thank me later

Posted by: Commander Goose, Protector of the Living Room

At exactly 3:14 AM, I sensed... a vibe.

Could've been a moth. Could've been a ghost. Could've been the crushing weight of capitalism.
But I barked. Oh, I barked with purpose.

She told me to hush. She mumbled something about "sleep."
Sleep? Sleep is for people who don't believe in safety.

I spun in three angry circles and flopped down loudly.
But I kept one eye open. Watching. Judging. Guarding.

And when morning came?
No ghosts.
You're welcome.
